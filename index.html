The Power Learn Project (PLP) is a Pan-African initiative aimed at bridging the digital skills gap in Africa by offering fully funded software development scholarships. The organization focuses on democratizing tech education, fostering innovation, and promoting sustainable development. It provides learners with the tools needed for success in the global tech ecosystem, empowering youth through employment and innovation. The team includes professionals with expertise in education, strategy, finance, and partnerships.
